Dores 应用 技术支持与服务说明

最近更新日期：2025 年 12 月 9 日

一、适用范围

本说明适用于：

  • 产品名称：Dores 应用
  • 运行平台：macOS 14 及以上版本
  • 获取渠道：Mac App Store

二、提供的技术支持内容

1. 安装与启动问题

  • 应用无法安装或打开；
  • 菜单栏图标不显示；

2. 基础功能问题

  • 模型列表无法加载或菜单状态不刷新；
  • 快捷键配置无效或行为与预期不符；
  • 聊天窗口无法打开、无法发送消息或无响应。

3. 网络与 VIP 相关问题

  • 无法连接聊天或 VIP 服务器；
  • 试用领取失败或试用状态显示异常；
  • 内购支付成功后未解锁 VIP、恢复购买失败等。

4. 崩溃与严重错误

  • 应用频繁崩溃、卡死；
  • 长时间无法连接服务器、频繁弹出错误提示。

5. 其他合理范围内的技术问题

  • 版本更新、兼容性与性能相关的咨询；
  • 使用体验与功能建议（我们会根据情况用于后续优化）。

三、不属于技术支持范围的情况

在不违背法律法规及用户基本权益的前提下，以下情况原则上不在技术支持范围内：

  • 对应用进行破解、注入第三方插件、逆向工程等造成的问题；
  • 使用非官方渠道获取的安装包、修改版应用导致的问题；
  • 因操作系统严重损坏、系统设置异常、网络环境极端不稳定等外部原因导致的问题；
  • 与本应用无直接关系的第三方软件或硬件问题；
  • 超出一般用户支持范围的定制化需求（如深度定制開發等）。

四、如何获取技术支持

推荐通过电子邮件联系我们，以便记录问题和跟踪处理进度。

五、响应时间与处理流程

1. 响应时间

  • 一般情况下，我们会在 3–5 个工作日内给予初步回复；
  • 节假日或反馈量较大时，响应时间可能有所延长。

2. 处理流程

  • 问题受理：确认已收到您的反馈，如有需要可能向您索取更多信息（如日志、截图等）；

Dores Menubar App Technical Support Statement

Last updated: December 9, 2025

1. Scope

This document applies to:

  • Product name: Dores Menubar App (Dores Swift Menubar)
  • Platform: macOS 14 or later
  • Distribution channels: Mac App Store

2. What We Support

2.1 Installation and Launch Issues

  • Unable to install or open the App;
  • Menubar icon not showing;

2.2 Core Feature Issues

  • Model list not loading or menu status not updating;
  • Shortcuts not working or behaving unexpectedly;
  • Chat window cannot be opened, cannot send messages or is unresponsive.

2.3 Network and VIP Issues

  • Unable to connect to chat or VIP servers;
  • Trial activation fails or trial status is incorrect;
  • VIP not unlocked after successful purchase or restore purchase fails.

2.4 Crashes and Critical Errors

  • Frequent crashes or freezes;
  • Long-term inability to connect to servers or repeated error dialogs.

2.5 Other Reasonable Technical Questions

  • Questions about updates, compatibility and performance;
  • Suggestions regarding user experience and features (which may be considered for future improvements).

3. What Is Not Covered

Without prejudice to your statutory rights, the following are generally outside the scope of our support obligations:

  • Issues caused by cracking, unofficial plugins, reverse engineering or other unauthorized modifications;
  • Issues arising from unofficial builds, pirated or modified versions of the App;
  • Problems caused by severely corrupted operating systems, abnormal system settings, or extremely unstable networks;
  • Issues related to third-party software or hardware that are not directly associated with the App;
  • Highly customized requests beyond general end-user support (such as bespoke development).

4. How to Get Support

4.1 Email Support

Email is the recommended way to contact us, as it allows us to track and follow up on your case.

  • Email: doresdev2025@163.com
  • Suggested subject: [Dores Support] + short description

Please include the following information where possible:

  • Your system version (e.g. macOS 14.x);
  • The App version (see “About” or app info);
  • Your user ID (you can find it in the “User Info” menu item in the menubar);
  • Steps to reproduce the issue (as detailed as possible);
  • Relevant screenshots or screen recordings, if available.

4.2 Other Channels (If Any)

If we later provide additional channels such as a website ticket system, social media accounts or live chat support, we will announce them in the App or on our website.

5. Response Time and Process

5.1 Response Time

  • In general, we aim to provide an initial response within 3–5 business days after receiving your request;
  • During holidays or periods of high load, response times may be longer.

5.2 Handling Process

  • Acknowledgement: confirm receipt of your report and request additional information if necessary;
  • Investigation: our engineers analyze and attempt to reproduce the issue;
  • Resolution: provide workarounds or usage guidance, or fix the issue in a future release and describe it in the release notes.

6. Updates to This Statement

We may update this Technical Support Statement from time to time based on changes in features or service capabilities. Updated versions will be made available in the App or through relevant channels. Your continued use of the App or continued requests for support will constitute acceptance of the updated Statement.
