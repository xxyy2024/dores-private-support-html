Dores 应用 隐私政策

最近更新日期：2025 年 12 月 9 日

欢迎使用 Dores 应用（以下简称“本应用”或“我们”）。我们非常重视您的个人信息和隐私保护。 本隐私政策旨在向您说明我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的信息，以及您所享有的权利。

当您使用本应用时，即表示您已阅读、理解并同意本隐私政策的全部内容。

一、我们收集的信息

我们仅在实现核心功能、保障服务安全和改进体验所必需的范围内收集信息。

1. 您主动提供的信息

  • 聊天内容：您在聊天窗口中输入的文本，用于调用后端大模型或其他智能服务生成回复。
  • 反馈与联系信息：当您通过邮箱或其他方式联系我们时，可能会提供昵称、邮箱地址以及问题描述、截图等。

2. 与账号和支付相关的信息

  • Apple 账号相关标识：在需要时，我们可能通过 Apple 提供的接口获取与您的 Apple ID 关联的匿名标识 （例如 userIdentifier 等），仅用于识别用户、恢复购买和防止欺诈。
  • 交易信息：通过 StoreKit 内购时，我们会接收 Apple 提供的交易凭证（如订单号、 originalTransactionId、appAccountToken 等），用于验证交易真实性、解锁或恢复 VIP 权益。

我们不直接获取也不会保存您的银行卡号、支付密码等敏感支付信息，相关信息仅由 Apple 及其支付渠道处理。

3. 设备与日志信息

  • 设备信息：包括设备型号、操作系统版本、应用版本、语言设置等，用于适配界面和定位问题。
  • 运行与错误日志：本应用会在本地生成调试日志（例如写入应用沙盒目录下的日志文件），记录应用运行状态、 网络连接情况、部分错误信息等，用于排查问题和改进性能。日志默认仅保存在本地，不会自动上传到服务器， 只有在您主动向我们发送或授权上传时，我们才会获取相关内容。

4. 网络请求信息

  • IP 地址、请求时间、错误码等基础网络信息，用于保障网络服务安全稳定。
  • 用于聊天与 VIP 校验的必要信息（如经脱敏处理的用户标识、聊天文本、VIP 状态等）。

二、我们如何使用您的信息

  • 提供核心功能：实现桌面伴侣显示、模型切换、快捷键控制、聊天对话、VIP 解锁与恢复等功能。
  • 保障安全与稳定：通过日志与监控发现故障、防止恶意攻击或滥用，并优化性能。
  • 产品优化与统计：基于去标识化或匿名化的数据，分析功能使用情况并改进产品体验。
  • 符合法律法规：根据法律法规或有权机关要求，在合法合规的前提下使用或披露必要信息。

三、信息的存储与保护

1. 存储方式与期限

  • 本地存储：应用运行日志、缓存等存储在您的设备本地，您可通过系统工具或卸载应用进行删除。
  • 服务器存储：与账号、购买、聊天相关的必要信息会存储在服务器，用于同步状态与提供服务。
  • 保存期限：我们仅在实现本政策所述目的所必需的期限内保存数据，超出期限后将删除或匿名化处理， 法律法规另有要求的除外。

2. 安全措施

  • 我们采用合理可行的安全措施保护您的数据，防止未经授权的访问、披露、使用、修改或损坏。
  • 我们仅允许有必要知晓这些信息的人员在最小权限原则下访问，并要求其履行保密义务。

3. 数据的跨境传输

如服务部署在境外服务器，可能涉及数据跨境传输。我们会遵守适用法律法规的要求，并采取措施保障数据安全。

四、我们如何共享、转让或公开披露信息

1. 共享

除以下情形外，我们不会与任何公司、组织或个人共享您的个人信息：

  • 实现核心功能所必需：
    • 与 Apple 共享内购相关的必要信息，用于分发应用、完成支付和验证交易。
    • 与第三方大模型/推理服务提供方共享必要信息（如聊天内容及上下文），用于生成智能回复。
  • 在获得您明确同意的情况下，与指定的第三方共享。
  • 根据法律法规或有权机关要求提供。

2. 转让

我们不会将您的个人信息转让给其他公司、组织或个人，除非获得您的明确同意，或在公司合并、收购、重组等情形下， 我们会要求新的持有方继续受本隐私政策约束，否则将重新征得您的授权同意。

3. 公开披露

我们不会公开披露您的个人信息，除非获得您的明确同意，或为遵守法律法规、履行强制性义务所必需。

五、您的权利

在法律法规允许的范围内，您对自己的个人信息享有以下权利：

  • 访问与更正：您可以通过应用或联系方式请求访问、更正您的部分信息。
  • 删除：在满足法律规定条件时，您可以要求我们删除您的个人信息（例如处理目的已实现或不再必要等）。
  • 撤回同意：您可以通过系统或应用设置关闭相关权限，或联系我们撤回某些处理活动的同意。
  • 账号相关权利（如适用）：如未来提供账号系统，您可申请导出信息副本或注销账号，我们会在符合法律要求的前提下处理。

六、未成年人保护

本应用面向具有完全民事行为能力的成年用户。若您是未成年人，请在监护人指导下使用本应用，并建议由监护人阅读本隐私政策。

七、本隐私政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。当条款发生重大变化时，我们会通过应用内显著位置提示或更新说明告知您。 如您在变更后继续使用本应用，即视为您已接受修改后的政策。

八、联系我们

如您对本隐私政策或个人信息保护有任何疑问、意见或建议，或希望行使上述权利，可通过以下方式联系我们：

Dores Menubar App Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 9, 2025

Welcome to the Dores Menubar application (the “App”, “we”, “our” or “us”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we store and protect it, and what choices and rights you have.

By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood and agreed to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide core functionality, maintain security and stability, and improve the user experience.

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Chat content: Text you type in the chat window, used to call backend models or other intelligent services to generate responses.
  • Feedback and contact details: When you contact us (for example, via email), you may provide a nickname, email address, and problem details or screenshots.

1.2 Account and Payment Information

  • Apple account identifiers: When needed, we may obtain anonymous identifiers associated with your Apple ID (such as userIdentifier) via Apple-provided APIs, solely for user recognition, purchase restoration and fraud prevention.
  • Transaction information: For in-app purchases via StoreKit, we receive transaction receipts from Apple (such as order numbers, originalTransactionId, appAccountToken, etc.) to verify purchases and unlock or restore VIP access.

We do not directly access or store your bank card numbers, payment passwords or other sensitive payment information. Such data is handled by Apple and its payment providers.

1.3 Device and Log Information

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, app version, language settings, and similar technical details.
  • Runtime and error logs: The App may write diagnostic logs locally (for example to a log file under the app sandbox directory), recording runtime status, network connectivity and error messages to help troubleshoot issues and improve performance. Logs are stored on your device by default and are not uploaded automatically. We only receive them if you choose to share or upload them when seeking support.

1.4 Network Request Information

  • Basic network data such as IP address, request timestamps and error codes to maintain the security and stability of our services.
  • Necessary request data for chat and VIP validation (for example, de-identified user IDs, chat text, VIP status).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core features: Desktop companion display, model switching, keyboard shortcuts, chat conversations, VIP unlock and restoration.
  • Maintain security and stability: Detect and fix failures, prevent abuse or attacks, and monitor service performance.
  • Product improvement and analytics: Use de-identified or aggregated data to understand feature usage and improve the App.
  • Compliance: Fulfill legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from competent authorities.

3. Storage and Protection of Information

3.1 Storage Method and Retention Period

  • Local storage: Logs and cache files are stored on your device and can usually be removed by system tools or by uninstalling the App.
  • Server storage: Necessary account, purchase and chat information is stored on our servers to synchronize states and provide services.
  • Retention period: We retain information only for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. After that, we will delete or anonymize the data.

3.2 Security Measures

  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, use, modification or destruction.
  • Access to personal data is limited to personnel and partners who need it to perform their duties and who are bound by confidentiality obligations.

3.3 Cross-Border Data Transfers

If our servers are located outside your jurisdiction, your information may be transferred across borders. We will handle such transfers in compliance with applicable laws and will take appropriate safeguards.

4. Sharing, Transfer and Disclosure of Information

4.1 Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following situations:

  • Necessary for core functionality:
    • Sharing necessary purchase-related information with Apple to distribute the App, process payments and verify transactions.
    • Sharing necessary data (such as chat content and context) with third-party model/inference providers to generate responses.
  • With your explicit consent to share information with designated third parties.
  • When required by applicable law or by competent authorities.

4.2 Transfer

We do not transfer your personal information to other companies, organizations or individuals, except with your explicit consent or in connection with a merger, acquisition or restructuring. In such cases, we will require the new entity to be bound by this Privacy Policy or obtain your consent again.

4.3 Public Disclosure

We do not publicly disclose your personal information unless we have your explicit consent or are required to do so by law.

5. Your Rights

Subject to applicable laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and correction: You may request access to and correction of your information via the App or by contacting us.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your information where certain conditions are met (for example, when the purpose of processing has been fulfilled or is no longer necessary).
  • Withdraw consent: You may withdraw your consent to certain processing activities at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before withdrawal.
  • Account-related rights (if applicable): If we provide an account system, you may request export of certain data or account deletion, subject to legal requirements.

The App is intended for users who have full legal capacity. If you are a minor, please use the App under the guidance of your guardian, and ask your guardian to read this Policy.
